IMDOSOC

IMDOSOC is a non-profit educational organization based in Mexico City. They deliver resources such as books and courses on civic education as well as religion.


For this project I was an advisor for the organization.
I chose to get involved with IMDOSOC as I considered that my experience with Social Media and planning was a good fit for what they needed.

The Outcome:

At the time when we started the project, the marketing and customer success teams were isolated from each other. Throughout this project we managed to create a partnership between both teams by designing a customer journey that started with the social media promotions all the way to the remarketing efforts implementation.


CUSTOMER SERVICE

  • - Designed an actionable process to increase customer engagement.
  • - Created a management system that tracked clients throughout their user journey.

SOCIAL MEDIA

  • - Increased post frequency by 2x.
  • - Refined messaging and shifted focus to most effective media formats.
  • - 53% growth on organic reach (Q1 to Q3) in relation to 2019.

This project shows the importance of alignment within an organization. Once the social media and customer service teams knew their role in the client acquisition process they took action on their main tasks and managed to grow. Consistency with social media postings and client retention efforts was key. I got informed by the organization that they where having previous customers return for more purchases.
